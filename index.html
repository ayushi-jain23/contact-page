FORM & FUNCTION FOLLOWS INNOVATION
When we are talking about User Experience Design, we are actually talking about innovation.

The key to great product experience is in the answer to “how can we add value to users’ everyday lives and create a lasting impact on how they react to digital experiences?” Most product companies find a reasonably good solution to everyday problems that their users face. But a few companies don’t settle with the usual, the slightly better, the workable within budgets and time. They innovate. UX design is innovative problem solving - from conceiving brand new approaches to innovating in every little thing that makes up the product. And with the tech we have in our hands today, anything is possible and if at all we find ourselves limited, it's only in our imagination.
